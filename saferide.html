~ Saferide ~

Safe Ride is devoted to the idea that a person regardless of gender identity, race, religion, or sexual preference should be able to go where they wish at night while feeling comfortable and safe. Providing nearly 15,000 rides a year, Safe Ride is a free shuttle service that provides university students, faculty and staff with an alternative to walking alone at night.
